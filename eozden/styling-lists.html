Recipe (ordered) list

  1. Toast pitta, leave to cool, then slice down the edge.
  2. Slice up your chicken into small pieces and let marinate overnight in yogurt and a spice mix of your choice
  3. The next day skewer the chicken and get in contact with your local turkish person (Huseyin Emre Ozden (me)) and slow-cook over fire
  4. Fry the halloumi in a shallow, non-stick pan, until browned on both sides.
  5. Wash and chop the salad.
  6. Fill pitta with salad, humous, fried halloumi, and most importantly, THE CHICKEN
